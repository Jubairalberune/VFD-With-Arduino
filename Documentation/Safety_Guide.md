# ⚠️ Safety Guide

## 🔴 CRITICAL WARNING

**This project involves HIGH VOLTAGES (310V DC) that can be LETHAL!**

### General Safety Rules

1. **Treat everything as live** - Always check with multimeter
2. **Use one hand** - Keep one hand in pocket when working
3. **Isolate before working** - Unplug and wait 5 minutes
4. **Use safety equipment** - Safety glasses, insulated tools

### Discharge Procedure

1. Unplug the circuit
2. Wait 5 minutes
3. Use 100kΩ resistor to short capacitor
4. Check voltage with multimeter
5. Only touch when voltage < 10V

### Work Area Checklist

- [ ] Clean, dry work area
- [ ] Good lighting
- [ ] Fire extinguisher nearby
- [ ] No distractions
